Crockpot Chicken and Rice

Another easy meal for the family. With vegetables included, this delicious meal is just what you need!

Ingredients

  • 4-6 chicken breasts
  • 1.5 cops long grain brown rice
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 2 carrots (dicedd)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 10.5 ounces cream of chicken soup (one can)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • parsley (for garnish)
  • salt and pepper as preferred

How to make it

  1. Add all of the ingredients except for the cheese and peas to the Crockpot
  2. Stir everything together
  3. Cover and cook on high for 3.5-4 hours
  4. Add the frozen peas and half of the shredded cheese
  5. Top with remaining cheese
  6. Cover and cook for 10 more minutes
  7. Enjoy!
