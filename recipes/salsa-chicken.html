Crockpot Salsa Chicken

This easy-to-make meal will be an instant family favorite. The simple recipe makes this an easy meal to whip up during your busy work week. Give it a shot and let me know what you think in the comments below!

Ingredients

  • 4-6 chicken breasts
  • 12oz red salsa
  • 12oz avocado salsa
  • shredded cheese
  • cilantro
  • lime

How to make it

  1. Place chicken breasts into crockpot
  2. Pour both salsas into crockpot and stir
  3. Cook on either high for 4-5 hours, or low for 6-8 hours, stirring occasionally
  4. Shred chicken once ready
  5. For garnish, add shredded cheese, squeeze of lime, and cilantro
  6. Enjoy!
